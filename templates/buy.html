What is the grid ?

Grid-Connected Solar Systems

In solar energy, the grid refers to the electrical power network that delivers electricity from power plants to homes and businesses. Grid-connected solar systems allow homeowners to generate their own electricity with solar panels, feeding any excess power back into the grid when their needs are met.

  • Homeowners can draw power from the grid when solar production is low (e.g., at night).
  • Excess solar power is sent back to the grid, earning credits through net metering.
  • Most systems rely on the grid without the need for battery storage.

This integration promotes clean, renewable energy use while offering cost-saving benefits to consumers.
